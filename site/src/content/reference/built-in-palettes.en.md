---
title: Built-in Palettes
---

AntV Infographic has the following built-in palettes, and will continue to update and expand them:

- AntV brand color (`antv`)
- `spectral`: A gradient palette suitable for displaying continuous data scenarios

You can get the complete list of built-in palettes through the [getPalettes](/reference/infographic-exports#get-palettes) method.

You can also get the specific color values or generation function of a palette based on its ID through the [getPalette](/reference/infographic-exports#get-palette) method.
