---
title: Built-in Themes
---

AntV Infographic has the following built-in themes, and will continue to update and expand them:

- Default theme (`default`): An infographic theme suitable for most scenarios, with a clean and elegant style.
- Dark theme (`dark`): An infographic theme suitable for dark backgrounds, improving visual contrast.
- Hand-drawn theme (`hand-drawn`): An infographic theme designed in hand-drawn style, suitable for relaxed and lively scenarios.

You can get the complete list of built-in themes through the [getThemes](/reference/infographic-exports#get-themes) method.

You can also get the specific configuration of a theme based on its ID through the [getTheme](/reference/infographic-exports#get-theme) method.

When you need to customize colors or styles, you can refer to [Custom Theme](/learn/custom-theme) for extension.
